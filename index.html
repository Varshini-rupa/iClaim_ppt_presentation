Slide 2 — The Challenge
THE HEALTHCARE BOTTLENECK
The High Cost of Operational Latency & Fraud Leakage in Health Insurance
"Meet Ramesh, a patient in Andhra Pradesh. After his hospital discharge, he waits an average of 15 days for his health insurance claim settlement. Why? Because underwriters are buried under manual document verification, checking doctor credentials, matching policy clauses, and scanning for fraud across hundreds of bills per day. Ramesh's wait is the human and business cost of manual claims processing."
🐢
Operational Latency
A prolonged settlement cycle driven by manual data entry and document verification delays, destroying customer NPS.
⚖️
Reviewer Fatigue
Reviewers process massive document loads daily, leading to burnout, inconsistent policy validation, and human error.
🚨
Fraud Leakage
Undetected duplicate bills and unverified credentials bypass fatigued reviewers, driving millions in annual financial losses.

Slide 5 — Core AI Engine
THE INTELLIGENCE: THREE-PILLAR CONVERGENCE
Combining OCR, Semantic RAG, and ML Fraud Detection
Document Intelligence (IDP)
OCR Extraction Pipeline
⚡ Tesseract OCR + PyMuPDF
Automatically parses unstructured medical bills and discharge summaries. Normalizes patient data, admission dates, and billing codes into structured schemas.
Policy Intelligence
Semantic RAG Pipeline
⚡ spaCy + SQLite VectorStore
Replaces heavy external DBs. Performs local cosine-similarity vector searches across policy documents to instantly validate coverage and exclusion criteria.

→

→

Risk Score 0-1.0
🤖 Decision AI — Assessment Generator
All pipelines converge to provide the underwriter with a unified recommendation, confidence score, and clear explainability.
Slide 6 — MVP Execution
MULTI-ROLE PORTALS: HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP
Delivering Tangible Value to Customers, Hospitals, and Reviewers
👤
Customer & Hospital Portals
Guided Claim Submission
  • Real-time completeness checks prevent incomplete claims.
  • Bilingual AI chatbot (Telugu & English) with speech-to-text.
  • Strict LLM guardrails (FastAPI) block prompt injections.
  • Live status tracking and policy status lookups.
🔍
Reviewer & Admin Hub
AI-Assisted Decision Support
  • AI vets coverage, exclusions, and generates a fraud risk score.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Reviewers hold full override power.
  • One-click Approve / Reject / Escalate workflows.
  • Comprehensive audit trails and analytics dashboard.
🧪 Enterprise QA & Testing
Jenkins CI/CD pipeline runs pytest on every push. Verifies claim lifecycle transitions, schema compliance, and fraud model integrity (5-fold cross-validation).
🌍 Production-Ready Resiliency
Zero-dependency local databases ensure continuous uptime. Mock fallbacks guarantee core system operability even if external APIs experience downtime.
